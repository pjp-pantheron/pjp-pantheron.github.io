What is Pantheron?

Pantheron is Pope John Paul II High School's annual charitable dance marathon. Through a partnership with three organizations, the funds raised by the event are donated to support pediatric causes.

In addition to direct donations, Pantheron is supported by a variety of events throughout the school year. Donut days, dine & donate events, and dress down days are just a few of the ways PJP students directly contribute to meeting the donation goal.
